The Beginning

The Outer Space began with the Big Bang about 13.8 billion years ago, when all matter, energy, space, and time suddenly expanded from an extremely hot, dense point. In the first moments, the universe rapidly inflated,cooled, and formed the first particles, which later combined into atoms,stars, and galaxies, creating the vast cosmos we see today.
